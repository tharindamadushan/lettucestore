About the Game

Satisfactory is a first-person open-world factory building game developed by Coffee Stain Studios. Explore an alien planet, gather resources, and build complex automated factories to progress through the game.

Features

  • Expansive open-world exploration
  • Complex factory building and automation
  • Multiplayer co-op with friends
  • Resource gathering and crafting
  • Regular updates with new content

System Requirements

Platform: Windows PC

Storage: Approximately 20 GB

Processor: Intel Core i5 or equivalent

Memory: 8 GB RAM

Graphics: Nvidia GTX 770 or equivalent

Internet: Required for multiplayer and updates
